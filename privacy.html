Privacy Policy

In compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 ("PDPA").

1. Introduction

HR Sage ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) of Malaysia.

2. Collection of Personal Data

We collect only the minimal data required to provide our services:

  • Identity Data: Name, Company Name, Job Title.
  • Contact Data: Email address, Phone number.
  • Financial Data: Processed directly via Stripe (we do not store card details).
  • Usage Data: Chat inputs and interaction logs required to generate AI responses.

3. Purpose of Processing

Your data is processed for the following purposes:

  • To provide the HR AI chatbot services and generate answers.
  • To process your subscription payments and invoicing.
  • To communicate with you regarding service updates or security alerts.

4. AI & Data Retention (Zero-Retention)

We distinguish between Account Data (kept for administrative purposes) and Query Data (your HR scenarios).

As stated in our Security Overview, your Query Data is processed in an isolated environment. We do not sell your data to third-party brokers, nor do we use your proprietary inputs to train public AI models.

5. Disclosure of Data

We may disclose your data to trusted third-party service providers solely for operational needs:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe (for billing).
  • Cloud Infrastructure: AWS (for hosting).
  • Legal Compliance: If required by Malaysian law or court order.

6. Your Rights

Under the PDPA, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (which may result in termination of service).

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at support@hrsage.ai.
