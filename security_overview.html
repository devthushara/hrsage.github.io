Security Center

Security Overview

We take the protection of your sensitive HR data seriously. Here is how we engineer trust into every layer of our platform.

1. Data Encryption

We employ bank-grade encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest, ensuring that your internal documents remain confidential.

  • In Transit: All data sent between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ (Transport Layer Security).
  • At Rest: Sensitive user data stored in our databases is encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption.

2. Payment Security (PCI Compliance)

We do not store your credit card information on our servers. All billing infrastructure is outsourced to the industry leader.

  • Payments are securely processed by Stripe.
  • Stripe is a certified PCI-DSS Level 1 Service Provider (the most stringent level of certification available).

3. AI & Data Privacy

We understand that you are sharing sensitive HR scenarios with our AI. Our architecture is designed as a Zero-Retention Vault.

Data Isolation

Your query data is processed strictly for the purpose of generating an answer in an ephemeral environment.

No Third-Party Training

We do not use your proprietary HR data or scenarios to retrain our public models. Your knowledge remains yours.

4. Infrastructure & Reliability

Our application is hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS), utilizing world-class data centers with 24/7 physical security.

We perform regular automated backups to ensure data integrity and availability.

Vulnerability Reporting

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in our platform, please let us know immediately so we can fix it.
