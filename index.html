Why HR Sage?

General LLMs are trained on the open internet, mixing US labor laws with global forums. HR SAGE is engineered strictly on Malaysian Statutes and Case Law.

The "Black Box" Risk

Using public AI tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude) for HR matters exposes your company to three critical failures:

Jurisdictional Confusion

General AI often applies US "At-Will Employment". In Malaysia, Security of Tenure is guaranteed.

Phantom Case Law

LLMs invent court cases. They may cite a "2023 Supreme Court Ruling" that never happened.

PDPA Violations

Pasting employee salary data or IC numbers into public chatbots violates the PDPA 2010.
